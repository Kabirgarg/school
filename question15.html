Computer gaming

A personal computer game (also known as a computer game or PC game) is a game played on a personal computer, rather than on a video game console or arcade machine.Computer games have evolved from the simple graphics and gameplay of early titles like Spacewarl, to a wide range of more visually advanced titles.

Basically there are four types of computer games:

  1. Strategy
  2. First Person Shooters
  3. 3rd Person Views
  4. Racing
